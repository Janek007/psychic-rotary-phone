How does this work?

Generate a deposit address and send your Bitcoin to the deposit address. There is no minimum.

After 2 confirmations your deposit will be confirmed and the Coin equivalent will be added to your account balance. You can then use your Coins anywhere on Cointiply.

If you have more than 35,000 Coins in your account you can earn 5% annual interest on your Coin balance.

Important: Deposits over $5,000 will be held for a minimum of 14 days before they can be withdrawn. They can be used for PTC ad purchases without delay.
